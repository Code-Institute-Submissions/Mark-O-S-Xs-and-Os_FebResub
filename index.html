X's and O's

A game of Tic Tac Toe

Tic tac toe is an easy and fun to play 2 player game (or vs a bot). "X" is the first player and "O" is the second player. The board is made up a 3 x 3 grid of squares, meaning that it has three rows of three squares. Each player takes turns and has one move per turn, they must mark the board with their letter. Whoever is the first that has 3 letters in a row is the winner!
